Helpful information about your visit

Directions(by car):

Heading North on the a12 – take the exit for Kelvedon (J23), continue on the B1024 through Kelvedon and Feering. Before the slip road to the a12 North there is a sign for Prested Hall; follow the road around to the driveway.

Heading South on the a12 – take the exit for Feering (J24) and turn left into the driveway to Prested Hall. The turning is on the slip-road, be ready to slow down substantially once off the a12.

Prested Hall has a long driveway. Follow it towards the main house and then turn left at the signs for the Health Club. Follow this around to the parking area.

On arrival:

Enter the Health Club through the double doors. The Health Club reception will buzz you through the gates and guide you to the seating area.

Other:

Mobile phones – to enjoy an uninterrupted treatment, please turn your mobile phones to silent.

Clothing – please wear loose comfortable clothing. There are male and female changing rooms available.
